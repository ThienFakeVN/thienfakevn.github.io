(New) Year of injuries

So I have a question: Have you ever had a cut on your leg, then fallen and got your knee wounded, and then fallen one more time and had to use a crutch to walk? If you say yes, then you had got the same situation with me in the literal start of this year (2025). So, what truly happened?

So, on the 5th of January, when I was walking, I suddenly touched my father's motorbike's footrest. I felt a pain, but I didn't think it was bad. And guess what — I was wrong. When I was watching the 2024 ASEAN Championship final, I looked down at my leg and oh shit I saw a scratch on my leg! After that, I just... rubbed oil on it and went to bed, not to sleep though. In the moment, I thought, «Ah no, it's just the beginning». And, however, this time I was CORRECT.

At the midnight between 24th and 25th of January, if you know, this was Tết of 2025. So, at the midnight of said days, before when the fireworks were being fired, normally, you would not like to go completely outside, it's dangerous, but in the moment, I decided to go out my safe spot and run back when the fireworks were fired. But when I was running — bam! — I felt an injured my knee. So I wasn't able to see the rest fireworks and I decided to not go anywhere — just sleep on my hammock.

But that wasn't the worst, when I went back to school, the students attended to a school's activity session, I sat right next to the student on whom I have a crush (let's go) but right in that moment, I look at my finger, and for some reason it was BLEEDING, WTF? But even that wasn't the worst. When the session ended, we had a plenty of free time, I was like yessir and running happily, and — BAM! — I felt and this time the area near my ankle was hurt a lot! I couldn't even walk normally. I painfully walked to and sat on a bench. Some of my friends saw me and each of them all recommended me to go to the medical room but I, partly thought my school's medical room seemed... suspicious, partly thought it would be normal so I decided to not go, even though my head was already generating images of me being confined on a wheelchair. The good new was, it didn't happen. The bad new was, a bone near my ankle was cracked, so I had to use a crutch to walk for nearly 2 month.

Well, that's the last disaster, which also ends this post
