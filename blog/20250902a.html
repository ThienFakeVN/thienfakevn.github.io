The Second of September has come!

Today is 02.09.2025.

This day, 80 years ago, in 1945, Hồ Chí Minh read the Declaration of independence of Vietnam at the Ba Đình Square in Hanoi, declared independence of Vietnam under the name of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Today, a military parade was held in the same place. The parade is a symbol of Vietnamese people's achievements in the past, pride and strength of the current Vietnam.

Chúc mừng ngày Quốc Khánh mùng 2 tháng 9/Happy the National Day on the Second of September/С Национальным Праздником Второго Сентября!
